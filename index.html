July 7, 2020: Immanuel Kant

This is a random German philosopher who was a professor at the University of Königsberg. He used the term schema to mean the way
a non-empirical concept is associated with the mental image of an object. Here are some of his works:

  • Critique of Pure Reason - best known work which was followed by Critique of Judgment and Critique of Practical Reason.
    Proposed that results from geometry and simple physics might be a potential synthetic a priori truths.
    The section “The Metaphysical Deduction” explains the evolution of categories (pure concepts of understanding).
    Statements such as "the soul is simple" and "the soul is a substance" are "paralogisms" of its title subject.
  • Perpetual Peace - a world without war based on constitutional republics
  • Groundwork of the Metaphysics of Morals - outlined the categorical imperative
